Ryan Bostaph

About Me

Currently enrolled at Kent State University, Ryan Bostaph is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a concentration in Computer Information Systems.

As an avid technology enthusiast, Ryan remains current with the latest advancements in the field. He actively assists individuals seeking technological support and possesses proficiency in various operating systems, including iOS, WatchOS, MacOS, and iPadOS.

Beyond his academic pursuits, Ryan has held leadership roles in his high school community. He served as a member of the National Honor Society and as a Display Content Editor, responsible for managing content displayed on a television screen in the school’s lobby, which disseminated announcements and pertinent information. Additionally, he served as the Social Media Coordinator for his high school class, overseeing the management of the Barnesville High School Class of 2023 (@OfficialBHS2023) Twitter (X) account.

Ryan’s professional experience extends to his involvement with the Non-Profit Youth and Educators Succeeding organization. He commenced his work with the organization in February 2021, initially as a Student Action Agent. During the summer of 2021, he secured an internship that spanned from June to August, followed by another internship for the 2021-22 academic year, which concluded in December 2021.

Ryan holds certifications through GenYES Ohio, a program administered by INFOHIO. He has also obtained the Google IT Support Professional Certificate and the OhioMeansJobs Readiness Seal, which he achieved through a Pre-Apprenticeship program with his local ITC.
